# Pardaraz-Interactive-Resume
This page is created to show the variety of projects Pardaraz Consultant Engineers has done since its establishment in 2001.
All the projects are in Iran and Pardaraz have no project overseas.
In general, it demonstrates the experiences Pardaraz's four departments have had through almost 3 decades. 
Urban Development department with various experiences in urban planning, urban designing, urban regeneration, regional planning and urban historical quarters conservation programmes.
Architecture department with legal allowance to design private, public and governmental buildings, and Traffic department with different projects regarding traffic and road networks in cities, are the other sectors of Pardaraz.
Along those departments, there is Building Supervision department that has diverse experiences around the country.
